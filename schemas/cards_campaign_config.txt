# Cards Campaign Configuration Taxonomy

## Overview
Three campaign tables representing different credit card marketing plays:
- **PCD** (Product Change/Upgrade) — offers to switch/upgrade card product
- **PLI** (Pre-approved Credit Limit Increase) — pre-approved CLI offers
- **TPA** (Third Party Acquisition) — new card application campaigns

## 11. Known Data Gaps

What is NOT captured in these tables:
- **Cost per offer** — only PCD has `channelcost`. PLI and TPA have no cost data
- **Creative content/messaging** — PLI has creative IDs but no content. PCD/TPA have nothing
- **Holdout period** — no field tracking minimum time between campaigns for same customer
- **Suppression rules** — no visibility into who was excluded and why
- **Priority/arbitration** — when campaigns compete for same customer, no data on how winner is chosen
- **External factors** — no market conditions, competitor actions, seasonality flags
- **Customer consent/preferences** — no channel opt-in/opt-out tracking visible
