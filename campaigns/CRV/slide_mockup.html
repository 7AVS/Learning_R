Campaign Performance: Credit Card Installment Trigger [CRV]
Stop CRV on the IM online banner — audience saturated, ~42K PCL conversions recoverable
Analysis of 4.4M PCL-on-IM events with CRV exposure (May 2025 – Mar 2026). PCL clients on the IM online banner split by CRV exposure status: Action (exposed), Control (CRV-eligible, held out from deployment), and Not in CRV (PCL clients not in CRV's audience).
Cards Installment redeploys repeatedly on the same audience. By Q1 2026, 65% of contacts are hitting clients already touched 6+ times since Oct 2024, and response has dropped below 1.2%.
CRV contact frequency and response rate (Jan – Mar 2026 deployments, IM)
For PCL clients sharing the IM online banner with CRV, response is materially lower than for comparable clients held out from CRV exposure.
PCL response rate by CRV exposure status (May 2025 – Mar 2026, IM online banner)
PCL response: 15.02% when CRV is exposed, 15.99% when CRV is held out (same audience).
The 0.97 pp gap × 4.4M leads ≈ ~42K conversions recoverable if CRV stops.
