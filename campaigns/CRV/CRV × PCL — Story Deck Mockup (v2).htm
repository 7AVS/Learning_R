Discontinue CRV on the IM online banner — the PCL we recover is worth far more than the CRV we give up

CRV × PCL cannibalization · bulletproof re-analysis · Oct 2024 – Apr 2026
Decision asked of this room: approve discontinue (or throttle) of CRV on the mobile banner
Executive summary
Running CRV on the mobile banner suppresses a higher-value product; stopping it is net positive
  • It's real & causal. PCL converts 1.08pp lower when CRV is co-present (17.2%→16.2%), significant in 17 of 20 months on a valid randomized holdout.
  • It's genuine loss, not a swap. ~72% of recovered PCL is net-new demand; only ~28% would have been a CRV→PCL switch.
  • The trade favours PCL. PCL recovered at $675/conversion dwarfs CRV forgone at $36/installment — even counting every installment plan we'd lose.
  • Soft landing exists. Harm concentrates in high PCL-propensity clients, so a throttle captures most of the value.

Targeting & robustness
Cannibalization is top-heavy in PCL-propensity, so a throttle is a credible soft landing — and the effect survives every robustness check
Read with care: these are PCL-propensity deciles — we do not have a CRV decile. We target the throttle by where PCL is most cannibalized (the right lever); we make no per-decile CRV-value claims.
