Why one version of this came back blank and the other was full

Per-month cap — DEAD lever

"Max 2–5 CRV per month"

The customer only gets ~1 CRV touch a month to begin with. So "no more than 2 per month" never trims anything — every month is already under the cap. That's why that run was all zeros. Nothing to throttle.

Duration cap — REAL lever

"First N months of CRV, then stop"

Counting a customer's touches across all months piles up (1,2,3…8). Capping there cuts the later touches — exactly the ones still colliding with limit-increase offers. That's the version with real volume, and it's the one we kept.

Same customer, same touches — the only difference is whether you count per month (resets to 1 every month → nothing to cut) or cumulatively (climbs → later touches get cut). Drag the slider to 0 above and it's a full kill; slide to 8 and it's business-as-usual. Everything in between is the throttle.
