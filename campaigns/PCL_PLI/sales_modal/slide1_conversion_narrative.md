## Significance callout (the blank space — lay terms, no symbols)
The lift is statistically significant, and it stays significant even at a strict, conservative

positive result nearly every time.

## Bottom text box — value + interim + call (ONE block)
**Interim read — the results are real, the campaign isn't finished.**
Adding the Sales Modal on top of business-as-usual has already driven **~9,350 incremental
responses — about $4.4M in 5-year NIBT** (at $475 per response). The lift is statistically
significant and holds even at a conservative confidence bar.
These are **not final numbers**: the campaign is still in-flight and well short of its full
response window, so we are reporting what the data shows today — not a guaranteed endpoint. The
trend is positive, and as later responses mature the value is expected to rise, not fall.
**Call: the Sales Modal is working. Keep it running to completion and prepare to scale.**
